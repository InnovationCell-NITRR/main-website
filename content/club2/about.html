AWARDS & RECOGNITION

The Award of Recognition in the Atal Innovation Ranking is given to the Innovation Cell of National Institute of Technology (NIT) Raipur for their outstanding contributions in promoting innovation and fostering a culture of entrepreneurship. This recognition highlights the cell's efforts in encouraging and supporting innovative ideas and projects, and its impact on the local community and society at large. The award is a testament to the hard work and dedication of the Innovation Cell in promoting innovation and driving progress.

This is to certify that National Institute of Technology, Raipur is categorized as "Band A' institution (rank between 11-25) in category of Institute of National Importance, Central Universities & CFTIs in Atal Ranking of Institutions on Innovation Achievement (ARIIA) 2020 announced on 18 Aug 2020.
